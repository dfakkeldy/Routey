Routey helps rural carriers turn a route plan into a calmer delivery day. Built for dead zones and parcel-heavy mornings, it keeps the working route local on your iPhone so sorting, lookup, and checkoff stay fast when service is spotty.

Routey is built around a simple flow: sort -> snap -> deliver.

KEY FEATURES

- Offline-first route planning: keep delivery work available from a local source of truth.
- Snap-to-add parcels: use on-device OCR to capture parcel-label details and match them to stops.
- Delivery-order checkoff: sort today's parcels into route order and work through the run without paper notes.
- Master route lookup: search addresses, delivery points, notes, and flags from one carrier-focused workspace.
- Flexible views: switch between parcel-only focus and the full route when the day gets messy.

HOW IT WORKS

Build or import a generic route, add today's parcel workload, then use Routey to organize the run in delivery order. The app is designed for rural delivery realities: shared boxes, clustered delivery points, patchy reception, and fast decisions from the truck.

Routey is not a carrier system and does not replace official delivery requirements. It is a personal logistics tool for organizing your own workflow.
